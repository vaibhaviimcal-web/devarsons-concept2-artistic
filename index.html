Who We Are

Over a century of excellence in specialty colorants

Established in 1923, the Devarsons group was established by the founding fathers as an application oriented manufacturer of dyes. The focus since inception was on being a "solution provider" rather than a dye supplier. Manufacturing was established in 1960-70 with Solvent Yellow 14 and Solvent Red 24, targeting Cosmetic and Petroleum industries.

Devarsons is an ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and OHSAS 18001 accredited company, developing and producing specialty colorants. With over 50 years of accumulated experience in Color Application, our product range is specifically designed to cater to customers in various industries such as Cosmetics, Crop Protection, Plastics, Petroleum, etc.

The emphasis within the organization is on developing new molecules or customizing current known molecules to meet highly specialized applications. Devarsons does not produce any one class of dye but rather an assortment of dyes with different chemistries to suit highly evolved applications.
